My Ghost Blog

As a self-taught developer, one of the biggest regrets I have was not documenting my work by means of a blog. I was constantly seeking knowledge, and felt as though I was not skilled enough to give advice. Now, however, I haved reached the point in where I believe I can truly provide information that can be wholly benefical to those striving to learn about different technologies in web development. Therefore, this blog will consist of short, person pieces about my experiences with different technologies, as well as articles that I have had published on SitePoint.com. That said, I recently rekindled my position at SitePoint after dedicating a few months towards gaining certain knowledge regarding development. In turn, I am now extremely confident in my work as well as my writing, and I am excited to see what is to come.
